Ir. Soekarno born in Surabaya, East Java, 06 June 1901. He is President The firts Republic of Indonesia was approved in the period 1945 - 1967. He was a figure of struggle who played an important role in freeing the Indonesian people from the Dutch colonialism. He was the proclamator of Indonesian Independence (together with Mohammad Hatta) which took place on 17 August 1945. Sukarno was the first to spark the concept of Pancasila as the basis of the Indonesian state and he himself named it.

Here's a time line of Ir. Soekarno's life:

  • 1901 - Soekarno is born, 6 June 1901 in Surabaya with the name Kusno Sosrodihardjo.

  • 1916 - During his school years he just went to local elementary school and also his high school in 1916. His friends in the schools are all Dutch.

  • 1920 - He is fall in love with Siti Oetari and they married at 1920.

  • 1945 - Soekarno together with Bung Hatta declare Independence from the Dutch and other countries for Indonesia. He did it at 10 am in the morning in front of 500 people in the Gedung Proklamasi. 18 August 1945 He became the first president of Indonesia.

  • 1970 - Seokarno dead in the aged 69 in Jakarta, Indonesia. His dead is because he and many senior army generals are kidnapped and killed by the 30 September Movement (G30S).
